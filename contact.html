About

Why do we love chocolate?

The answer could be simple: because it has an excellent taste, but it is more complex than that. Chocolate truly has a divine taste, regardless of the variety, but the real reasons why we like it so much are related to the chemical reactions it causes in the body. Thus, it has been demonstrated that breast milk has the closest sugar-fat ratio to chocolate, hence our preferences for this dessert. In addition, thanks to the chemical compounds in the cocoa beans, chocolate relaxes us, gives us a good mood and even helps the brain to a great extent.
